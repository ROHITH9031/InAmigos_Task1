Founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered NGO headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh — operating with transparency, compassion, and purpose.

Section 8 NGO 80G & 12A CSR-1 Registered NITI Aayog ISO 9001:2015

Our Initiatives

Six Projects, One Mission

Each project targets a distinct dimension of social inequality, working together to create holistic change in communities across India.

📚
Project BachpanShala
Nurturing Young Minds

Providing quality education to underprivileged children through informal learning centres and academic support, ensuring no child is denied the right to learn.

🍽️
Project Sewa
Serving Humanity

Regular distribution of food, clothing, and basic essentials to the underprivileged and homeless — because compassion starts with the basics.

🦅
Project Udaan
Women Empowerment

Skill development, financial literacy, and entrepreneurship programs equipping women with tools for independence and economic freedom.

🐾
Project Jeev
Animal Welfare

Rescue, rehabilitation, feeding, and protection of stray and injured animals — because every life, human or animal, deserves dignity.

🌿
Project Prakriti
Environmental Conservation

Tree plantation drives, awareness campaigns, and sustainability initiatives to restore and protect our natural environment for future generations.

🛠️
Project Vikas
Skill Development

Vocational training and employability enhancement programs that bridge the gap between potential and opportunity for India's youth.
